About Me

My Goal

My personal goal is to help as many people as possible with their technology needs. I am passionate about the intersection of human connection and technical efficiency.

My Philosophy

I love solving problems—including the ones that don’t strictly "need" to be solved. My personal slogan is: “If it isn’t broke, do fix it.” I believe in continuous improvement and optimization rather than just maintenance.

Persistence & Dedication

I pride myself on my persistence. Whether it's staying late at work to ensure plant orders are completed or spending hours at home troubleshooting a custom operating system on an old Google Pixel, I see my projects through to the end. I’ve even reinstalled Windows multiple times just to explore different versions and find the perfect configuration.

Community & Communication

I enjoy using my public speaking skills to share my passions. From serving on the Student Council in Middle School to helping my grandma stop a flood of spam notifications, I am never afraid to step in and communicate technical solutions clearly and effectively.
